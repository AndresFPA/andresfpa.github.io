Experience

Postdoctoral Researcher

KU Leuven

I am currently extending my doctoral research to develop a mixture-based SEM method for longitudinal panel data. This method addresses the challenge of comparing many groups by applying a clustering algorithm (i.e., mixture model) that not only finds subsets of similar groups but also explicitly models the temporal transitions and potential changes in cluster membership across time points.
